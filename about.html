About

I am a Computer Science student at SMU with hands-on experience in full-stack web development, AI research, and data-driven software engineering. I have built scalable applications using Java, C++, Python, JavaScript, and Node.js, and contributed to research projects focused on AI-driven audio annotation and speech dataset development.

My journey in technology began with a curiosity about how digital systems function, which evolved into a passion for creating intelligent, secure, and user-centered solutions. I enjoy bridging engineering fundamentals with practical problem-solving to build meaningful technology!

When I'm not coding, I actively explore emerging technologies and work on personal projects that challenge my creativity and technical versatility. I am also coordinating student organizations and guiding interdisciplinary teams to improve collaboration and engagement.

I'm driven by the belief that innovative software and strong community leadership can create meaningful real-world impact, and I continuously seek opportunities to expand my skills and contribute to mission-focused projects.
